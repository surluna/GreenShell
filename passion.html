My Passion

Hi, I am Ziwei. I have a strong interest in web development and have previously created a few small scale so-called "toy projects". However, I've always aspired to gain a deeper understanding of web design and to develop a real industrial-grade project. I hope that through this course and the construction of this project, I can not only enhance my technical skills but also positively influence my future career path in web development.

I enjoy online shopping in my daily life and have had an internship experience at an e-commerce platform, giving me some insight into the workings of e-commerce. At the same time, I am deeply concerned about our planet's environment. I always try my best to protect the environment and use eco-friendly products. I've noticed a lack of mainstream shopping sites focusing on eco-friendly products, which inspired me to create this project.

This platform is more than just a shopping site, it's a place to realize our vision. Our goal is to provide a convenient and reliable shopping option for consumers who wish to protect the earth. Here you can easily find a variety of eco-friendly products, saving time in selection while fulfilling our shared eco-friendly vision, contributing to the ecological harmony of our planet.

My website slogan is:

Go Green, Live Clean.
